Main highlights 
 
For the 12th August 2014 a total of 26 new samples were received by the Kenema Laboratory Kenema 21 Port 
Loko  3and  Bo  2Eleven  11  of  these  samples  are  positive  from  Kenema  9  and  Port  Loko  2Four  4  of  the 
remaining  samples are negative from Kenema 3 and Port Loko 1 while 11 samples arrive late and are recorded as 
pending Lab resultsee table 1 
 
The 4 previous Lab pending results from Kenema3 and Tonkolili 1 are all negative 
 
The cumulative number of Laboratory confirmed cases are 717  whiles confirmed death is 264with a Case Fatality 
Rate CFR based on confirmed cases of 368 
 
Fifty-three 53 patients are currently admitted at the Kenema Isolation Unit with no report received from Kailahun 
Isolation Unit Cumulative discharge is191
