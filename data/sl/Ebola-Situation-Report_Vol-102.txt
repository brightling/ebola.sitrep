Main highlights 
 
For the 6th September 2014 a total of 84 new samples were received and analysed by the CDC Kenema amp South 
African NICD MLU Western Area Laboratory Kenema 11 Kono 4 Koinadugu 2 Bombali 20 Tonkolili 3 Port 
Loko 16 Bo 3 Moyamba 7 and Western Area 18 Forty-two42 of these  samples are positive 35 are negative 
while 7 suspected cases have been recommended for a second test base on their symptoms onset
See table1

 
 
The cumulative number of Laboratory confirmed cases are 1276 whiles confirmed death is 426 with a Case Fatality Rate CFR 
based on confirmed cases of 334 
 
The increase in cumulative deaths is as a result of the data cleaning going on 
 
Sixty-seven 67 patients are currently admitted at the Kenema Isolation Unit with no report received from the Kailahun Isolation 
unit  
 
Cumulative discharge is still 268 
 
DMOs DSOs amp clinicians are reminded to read the comment on the daily Laboratory result for your action 
 
Data cleaning is on-going therefore the figures in table 1 will keep on changing as the cleaning continues
